## üí° Recommendations

### Option 1: Keep Current Theme

**Best if:**

- You prefer higher contrast
- Traditional green feels more trustworthy
- You like the dynamic progress bar colors
- Accessibility is top priority

### Option 2: Switch to Proposed Theme

**Best if:**

- You want a more modern look
- Category color-coding appeals to you
- You prefer softer, cleaner aesthetics
- You want a more systematic color system

### Option 3: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)

**Combine the best of both:**

- ‚úÖ Keep current green (`#16a34a`) for better contrast

- ‚úÖ Use proposed softer shadows
- ‚úÖ Keep current dynamic progress bars
