## 🔒 What's Locked in Free Tier?

### ❌ Hidden Features

1. **Total Signatures Stat** - Shows "•••" with lock icon
2. **Petition Updates** - Can't add updates to inform supporters
3. **Appeals System** - Can't appeal rejected petitions
4. **Contact Moderator** - Can't directly message moderators
5. **Multiple Images** - Limited to 1 image only

### ✅ What Free Users CAN Do

1. Create petitions up to 2,500 signatures
2. Collect signatures with phone verification
3. Use comments system
4. View "My Signatures" tab
5. Generate QR codes (FREE!)
6. Share on social media
7. Basic petition page
8. Email notifications

## 🎯 Which Tier is Right for You?

### Choose FREE if:

- 📍 Local community petition
- 👥 Small group (under 2,500 people)
- 💰 Budget is tight
- 🎯 Simple campaign
- ⏱️ Short-term petition

### Choose STARTER if:

- 🏘️ Neighborhood or town-wide
- 👥 Medium group (2,500-10,000)
- 📸 Need multiple images
- 📢 Want to send updates
- 💬 Need moderator support

### Choose PRO if:

- 🏙️ City or regional campaign
- 👥 Large group (10,000-30,000)
- 📊 Need detailed analytics
- ⚡ Want priority review
- 🎯 Serious about impact

### Choose ADVANCED if:

- 🇲🇦 National campaign
- 👥 Very large group (30,000-75,000)
- 📈 Need data export
- 🎨 Want custom branding
- 🏢 Organization/NGO

### Choose ENTERPRISE if:

- 🌍 Major national/international
- 👥 Massive reach (75,000-100,000)
- 🔌 Need API integration
- 🎨 Full custom branding
- 👔 Dedicated support needed

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Maximize Free Tier:

1. Keep target under 2,500 signatures
2. Use 1 high-quality image
3. Leverage comments for updates
4. Share QR code everywhere (it's free!)
5. Use social media extensively

### When to Upgrade:

1. ✅ Petition gaining traction
2. ✅ Need to inform supporters (updates)
3. ✅ Want to see total impact (stats)
4. ✅ Petition was rejected (appeals)
5. ✅ Need more images

### Smart Strategy:

1. **Start Free** - Test your petition idea
2. **Upgrade to Starter** - When you hit 1,000 signatures
3. **Upgrade to Pro** - When you need analytics
4. **Upgrade to Advanced** - For serious campaigns
5. **Upgrade to Enterprise** - For organizations
